Biography

Eicho Cablanakshulin, born on September 11, 1997, in Karshi, Uzbekistan, is a distinguished scientist and musician. His unique journey bridges cutting-edge research and profound musical artistry. Raised by his father, Zgētser Svankovachke, a Croatian businessman, and mother, Esma Veraeva, a dedicated physician, Eicho's path has been defined by intellectual rigor and creative exploration.
